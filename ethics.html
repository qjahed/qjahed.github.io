Ethics in Information Technology

IT ethics involve the responsible use of technology to protect users, ensure fairness, and promote transparency. Key guidelines include:

  • 💻 Developers should prioritize user privacy and security.
  • 📊 Data scientists must avoid bias and respect consent when handling data.
  • 🤖 AI and machine learning models should be explainable and transparent.
  • 🛡️ IT professionals should report vulnerabilities and avoid exploiting systems.

Sources: Ethics Resource Center
